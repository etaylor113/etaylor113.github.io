My Car Obsession



I'm a huge car nerd. I love going to car shows and seeing new styles and
modifications people have made to their cars to make them look better and perform more efficiently.

My favorite types of cars are modern muscle cars like the Camaro SS and Viper ACR.

European cars tend to be on the pricey side.
They favor smaller engines and a much more comfortable driving experience.

This makes the driver feel more at home in the cockpit.
