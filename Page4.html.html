Why I love the Gym!

I have always enjoyed challenging myself and finding new wasys to push past boundaries. Going to the gym helps me clear my mind after a rough day and its a healthy way to blow off some steam after a long work day. I also think it's important to stay in shape because being healthy makes you feel better and more energized!
